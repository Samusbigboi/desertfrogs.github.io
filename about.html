About Me

Who are you?

My name is Wilmer. I make games (slowly) and work with IT (constantly). This site is where I document both.

an artist and IT-technician passionate about creating, experimenting, and exploring new systems. My work spans from digital automation to creative projects that combine technology and art.

About my interests and hobbies

My journey bridges technology and creativity. I design workflows, build scripts, and tinker with systems like NixOS and n8n, as well as working solo-dev on a game. At the same time, I enjoy making visual art that explores form, color, and contrast.

I’m always curious, whether it’s testing a new programming language, experimenting with automation, or sketching concepts that merge technology and aesthetics.

Game dev
I am currently working, sparodically, on a game in the Godot engine. I'm doing all the work myself, some friends help here and there when they want. Design, 3D models, textures, particles, all me. However, I do use Claude for programming exclusively. The game is heavily inspired by the King's Field and Shadow Tower series. A slow paced, limited combat, first person, low poly, dungeon crawler. I have an itch.io site for the game that will be updated as the game progesses. The setting will be a dark fantasy world, with cosmic horror elements.

Short summary of my life

Milestone for my art - Art classes, wood & metal working, started programming

2017 – University of Skövde
Started studying game development, learning more about game development and group projects. Started using Linux

2020 – Tech repair
Started working with repairing phones and other similar tech

December 2nd, I started working at Axel Health, in the service team. I'm truly greatful for this chance to prove myself.
