About Me

I’m Wilmer Nyman — an artist and IT-technician passionate about creating, experimenting, and exploring new systems. My work spans from digital automation to creative projects that combine technology and art.

What I Do

My journey bridges technology and creativity. I design workflows, build scripts, and tinker with systems like NixOS and n8n for automation. At the same time, I enjoy making visual art that explores form, color, and contrast.

I’m always curious — whether it’s learning a new programming language, experimenting with automation, or sketching concepts that merge technology and aesthetics.
