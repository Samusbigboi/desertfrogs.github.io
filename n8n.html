NixOS - Connect N8N to Google Drive

This guide helps you connect your n8n to various Google Drive APIs, such as sheets, gmail, calendar. This is needed in order to automate various workflows, such as creating sheets and/or appending table rows o an existing sheet. It can also see and/or create various events on your calendar.


Prepare your n8n app, and paus during the step where you are prompted to connect to your Google Cloud API:

Go to Google Cloud Platform: Cloud Computing Services
Click on "Console" up on the right corner
Click on "Select a project" on up on the left corner
click on "New Project"
Name it > create
Go to the project, either through notification or "Select a project"
Open hamburger menu in top left
Click "APIs & Services" > Library > search for "google drive" > Enable
On the left, go to "OAuth consent screen" > Get started
Follow the wizard - use whatever credentials fit your project
Go to the "Audience" tab on the left > "Publish app"
Go back to home > "APIs & Services" > "Credentials" > "+ Create credentials" - "OAuth client ID > "Aplication Type" - Web application > Fill in name field > Under "Authorized redirect URIs" - "+ Add URI" > paste the URI (found on the n8n API connection page) > (HAVE NOT FINISHED)
